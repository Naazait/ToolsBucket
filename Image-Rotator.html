How to Rotate or Flip Your Image

  1. Upload Image: Click the "Select Image" button to choose a photo from your device.
  2. Rotate or Flip: Use the control buttons to adjust your image. 'Rotate' turns the image 90 degrees. 'Flip' creates a mirror image either horizontally (left-to-right) or vertically (upside-down).
  3. Choose Format: Select whether you want to save the final image as a PNG (best for transparency) or JPG (best for smaller file sizes).
  4. Download: Once you're happy with the result, click "Download Image" to save it to your computer or phone.
