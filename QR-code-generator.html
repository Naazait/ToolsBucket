  • Website URLs: Direct users to your website, blog post, social media profile, or any online content.

How to Easily Create Your Custom QR Code

Creating a QR code with our tool is designed to be straightforward and fast, even if you're a first-time user. Follow these simple steps to generate your perfect QR code:

  1. Input Your Data: Start by entering the information you want to encode into the QR code. You'll find a large text area labeled "Enter text, URL, or any other data." Here, you can type or paste anything from a website link, a short message, an email address, or even Wi-Fi credentials. The QR code preview will automatically update as you type, giving you instant feedback.
  2. Personalize Your Design: Once your data is entered, it's time to make your QR code truly yours.
    • Size Adjustment: Use the "Size (px)" slider to easily adjust the pixel dimensions of your QR code. This is crucial for ensuring readability, especially if you plan to print the code or display it on various screens. The current size in pixels is displayed next to the slider for precision.
    • Color Customization: Express your brand or preference by selecting custom colors. Our intuitive color pickers for "Dot Color" (the foreground) and "Background Color" allow you to choose any hue to match your specific needs, making your QR code visually appealing and distinctive.
    • Error Correction Level: Choose an "Error Correction" level from the dropdown menu (Low, Medium, Quartile, High). This setting determines how much damage or dirt the QR code can withstand while still being scannable. A higher level provides greater resilience but slightly increases the code's density. 'Medium' is suitable for most general uses.
  3. Download Your QR Code: After you've customized your QR code to your satisfaction, simply select your desired download format from the dropdown menu. You can choose between PNG (Portable Network Graphics) for high-quality images with potential transparency, or JPG (Joint Photographic Experts Group) for widely compatible, compressed images. Finally, click the "Download QR Code" button, and your custom QR code will be saved directly to your device, ready for immediate use.
