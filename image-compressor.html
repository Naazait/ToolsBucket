Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Why should I compress my images?

Compressing images is vital for several reasons. Firstly, it dramatically reduces file size, leading to much faster website loading times. Faster sites improve user experience, reduce bounce rates, and are favored by search engines, leading to better SEO. Secondly, it saves storage space on your devices and bandwidth when sharing or uploading.

Will compressing an image reduce its quality?

For lossy formats like JPG and WEBP, some data is removed during compression, which can lead to a reduction in quality. However, our tool provides a quality slider, allowing you to control this trade-off. You can often achieve significant file size reductions (30-70% or more) with minimal or imperceptible loss in visual quality. PNG compression, on the other hand, is lossless, meaning it reduces file size without losing any data, primarily by optimizing file structure.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No, absolutely not. Your privacy and security are our top priority. The entire image compression process takes place directly within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. This means your images never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete confidentiality.

What image formats does this tool support?

Our online image compressor is versatile and supports the most widely used image formats for the web: JPG (JPEG), PNG, and WEBP. This ensures you can optimize a broad range of your digital photos and graphics.

How much can I reduce image size without losing quality?

The extent of file size reduction without a noticeable quality drop largely depends on the original image's complexity, its format, and the compression 'quality' setting you apply. For most JPG and WEBP images, you can often reduce their size by 30% to 70% while maintaining excellent visual fidelity. Experiment with the quality slider to find the perfect balance for each of your images.

Is this image compressor really free?

Yes, our image compression tool is 100% free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscription fees, or limits on the number of images you can process. We are committed to providing accessible and useful tools to our users.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Currently, our tool is designed to process one image at a time, allowing for precise control and real-time preview of the compression results for each individual file. If you need to compress a large batch of images, you might consider using dedicated desktop software or a specialized online batch compressor.
