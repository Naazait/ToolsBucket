How to Crop Your Image

  1. Upload Your Image: Click the "Upload Image" button and select a photo from your computer or mobile device.
  2. Select the Area: A crop box will appear on your image. Click and drag the box to move it, or drag the corners and edges to resize it.
  3. Choose an Aspect Ratio: Use the buttons (e.g., 1:1 for a square, 16:9 for widescreen) to lock the proportions of the crop box. Select "Free" for a custom shape.
  4. Crop and Save: Once you are happy with the selection, click the "Crop & Download" button. Your new image will be saved to your device instantly.
