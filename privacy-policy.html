Privacy Policy for ToolsBucket

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

At ToolsBucket, accessible from www.toolsbucket.online, the privacy of our visitors is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by ToolsBucket and how we use it. Our core privacy principle is simple: **your files and data are yours and yours alone.**

1. Our "Client-Side Processing" Philosophy

The most important aspect of our privacy policy is that our tools are designed to work entirely on the "client-side." This means all file processing, conversions, and calculations happen directly within your web browser on your own computer. Your files are never uploaded, sent to, or stored on our servers. This ensures the highest level of privacy and security, as your data never leaves your device.

2. Information We Collect

  • Non-Personal Information: Like most websites, we collect non-personally-identifying information such as your browser type, device type, operating system, and language preference. This data is aggregated and used to understand how visitors use our website so we can improve our services.
  • Usage Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics services (like Google Analytics with IP anonymization) to gather anonymous statistics about which tools are popular and how they are used. This helps us optimize performance and decide which new tools to build. This data is always anonymized and cannot be used to identify you.
  • Personal Information (Optional): The only time we may collect personal information (like your name or email address) is when you voluntarily contact us via email or a feedback form. We only use this information to respond to your inquiries.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and tools.
  • To analyze usage trends and enhance the user experience.
  • To respond to your questions, comments, or support requests.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our services.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties.

4. Cookies

ToolsBucket may use cookies to improve your experience. A cookie is a small file stored on your computer that helps the site remember your preferences (like settings on a tool). You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. However, doing so may affect the functionality of some tools.

5. Data Security

Because we do not upload or store your files, the risk of a data breach related to your content is eliminated. For the limited anonymous and optional personal data we do collect, we implement reasonable security measures to protect it. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. ToolsBucket does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under 13. If you believe that your child has provided this kind of information on our website, please contact us immediately, and we will do our best to promptly remove it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.
