Understanding Meta Tags

Meta tags are invisible tags that provide data about your page to search engines and website visitors. While much of this data is not directly visible on the page itself, it plays a critical role in how your site is understood and displayed by search engines and social media platforms. Optimizing these tags is a fundamental part of on-page SEO.
