How Our OCR Tool Works

  1. Upload Image: Drag your image file onto the upload area, or click it to select a file from your device. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, BMP, and more.
  2. Select Language: Choose the primary language present in your image from the dropdown menu for the most accurate results.
  3. Automatic Conversion: The tool will automatically start the OCR process. You can see the live progress.
  4. Copy Text: Once finished, the extracted text will appear in the text box. You can then click "Copy Text" to copy it to your clipboard.
