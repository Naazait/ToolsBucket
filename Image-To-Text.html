Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is OCR (Optical Character Recognition)?

A: Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is a technology that converts different types of documents, such as scanned paper documents, PDF files, or images captured by a digital camera, into editable and searchable data. Essentially, it allows computers to 'read' text from images, turning pictures of words into actual, manipulable text characters. Our tool utilizes this advanced technology to perform its conversions.

Q: Is this Image to Text tool truly free?

A: Yes, absolutely! Our online OCR tool is completely free to use. You can convert as many images to text as you need without any cost, hidden charges, or limitations on the number of conversions. Our goal is to provide accessible and useful tools for everyone.

Q: Are my images and the extracted text secure and private?

A: Your privacy and security are our highest priorities. We are committed to protecting your data. All the processing, including the OCR conversion, takes place directly within your web browser. This means your images are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere online. Your data remains entirely on your device, ensuring maximum confidentiality and security.

Q: What image formats does the OCR tool support?

A: Our online Image to Text converter is designed to be highly versatile and supports a wide range of common image formats. You can upload files in formats such as JPG, JPEG, PNG, BMP, TIFF, and WebP. Simply choose your image file, and our tool will efficiently handle the text extraction process.

Q: How accurate is the text extraction from images?

A: The accuracy of the OCR technology is largely dependent on the quality of your input image. For clear, high-resolution images with crisp, well-defined text and good contrast against the background, you can expect a very high level of accuracy. However, factors like blurry images, low resolution, distorted text, complex backgrounds, unusual fonts, or poor lighting can reduce accuracy. We employ advanced OCR algorithms to maximize recognition rates even under less-than-ideal conditions.

Q: Does the converter support multiple languages for text recognition?

A: Yes, our OCR tool is multilingual! To ensure the most accurate results, you can select the primary language of the text present in your image from the provided dropdown menu. We currently support popular languages such as English, Hindi, Spanish, French, and German, and we are continuously working to expand our language support.

Q: Do I need to download or install any software to use this tool?

A: Absolutely not! Our Image to Text converter is a completely online, browser-based tool. There's no need for any software downloads, installations, or plugins. You can access and use it directly from your web browser on any device with an internet connection, whether it's a desktop computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

Q: Can this tool convert text from handwritten notes or documents?

A: While our OCR technology primarily excels with printed or typed text, it can attempt to convert handwritten notes or documents. However, the accuracy for handwritten text is generally lower and varies significantly based on the legibility, neatness, and style of the handwriting. For the best possible results with handwritten content, we recommend ensuring the handwriting is very clear, on a plain background, and well-lit.

Q: What can I do with the extracted text after conversion?

This allows you to effortlessly edit, search, translate, format, or repurpose the information that was previously inaccessible within an image.
