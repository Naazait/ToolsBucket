PDF to PowerPoint Converter

Transform your PDF documents into dynamic PowerPoint presentations effortlessly. Our free online converter takes each page of your PDF and turns it into a high-quality, full-page image on a separate slide in a PPTX file. It's the perfect way to present reports, lectures, or portfolios without needing to redesign them. The entire process is secure and private, happening right in your browser.

How It Works

  1. Select PDF: Drag and drop your file or click the upload area to choose a PDF.
  2. Convert: Click the "Convert to PPTX" button. If your PDF is password-protected, you will be prompted to enter the password.
  3. Download: Your PowerPoint presentation will be generated and the download will start automatically.
