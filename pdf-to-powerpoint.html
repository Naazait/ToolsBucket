How to Use Our PDF to PowerPoint Converter

Using our PDF to PPTX converter is incredibly straightforward, designed for efficiency and ease of access. Follow these simple steps to transform your documents:

  1. Select Your PDF File: You have two convenient options. You can either "Drag & drop" your PDF file directly into the designated upload area on the page, or click anywhere within the "Drag & drop" box to open your file explorer and manually "select a file" from your device.
  2. Initiate Conversion: Once your PDF is selected and its name appears, the "Convert to PPTX" button will become active. Click this button to start the conversion process. If your PDF is protected with a password, a prompt will appear asking you to enter it to proceed.
  3. Automatic Download: Our tool will swiftly process your PDF. You'll see status updates indicating the progress. Once the conversion is complete, your brand new PowerPoint presentation (in .pptx format) will automatically begin downloading to your device.
