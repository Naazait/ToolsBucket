About Keyword Density

Keyword density is a fundamental concept in on-page SEO. While its importance has evolved over the years, it's still a useful metric for ensuring your content is focused and relevant to your target topic without over-optimizing. The goal is to use your main keywords naturally within high-quality, user-focused content. Our tool provides the raw data, allowing you to make informed decisions about your content strategy.
