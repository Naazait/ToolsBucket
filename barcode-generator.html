About Barcode Formats

  • CODE128: A high-density code used widely in logistics and transportation for alphanumeric or numeric-only data.
  • EAN-13: The European Article Number, a 13-digit code used for marking retail products worldwide.
  • UPC-A: The Universal Product Code, a 12-digit code primarily used in the United States and Canada for retail products.
