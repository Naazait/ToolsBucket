Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is text reversal?

Text reversal is the process of reordering characters, words, or sentences in a backward sequence. For example, reversing "Hello" gives "olleH". Our tool offers various types of reversal, including character-by-character, word order, and even flipping text upside down.

Is my text secure when I use this tool?

Yes, your text is completely secure. All processing happens directly in your web browser. Your input text is never sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy and data security.

What's the difference between "Reverse Text" and "Reverse Wording"?

Reverse Text reverses every single character in the entire input string (e.g., "Hi there" becomes "ereht iH"). Reverse Wording reverses the order of the words themselves, but keeps the letters within each word in their original order (e.g., "Hi there" becomes "there Hi").

How does the "Flip Text (Upside Down)" option work?

This option uses special Unicode characters that visually resemble their upside-down counterparts. When you select this mode, the tool finds the corresponding flipped character for each letter/number in your text and reverses the overall order to create an upside-down effect.

Can I use this tool on my mobile phone?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. You can easily access and use the Text Reversal Tool on your smartphone or tablet, just as you would on a desktop computer.
