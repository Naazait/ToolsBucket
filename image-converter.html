About Image Formats

  • JPG (JPEG): Best for photographs. Uses lossy compression to create small file sizes.
  • PNG: Best for graphics, logos, and images requiring a transparent background. Uses lossless compression.
  • WebP: A modern format from Google that provides excellent compression for both photos and graphics, often creating smaller files than JPG and PNG.
