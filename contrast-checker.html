About WCAG Standards

  • AA Level (Minimum): Requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text. This is the most common accessibility target.
  • AAA Level (Enhanced): Requires a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text.
