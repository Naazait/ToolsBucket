Understanding Your EMI

The EMI is calculated based on a standard formula that takes into account the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. A longer tenure will result in a lower EMI but a higher total interest payment, while a shorter tenure increases the EMI but reduces the total interest paid over the life of the loan. Use our sliders to see how these factors impact your monthly payments.
