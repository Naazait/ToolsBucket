About Age Calculation

How It Works

Our age calculator computes the time difference between two dates: your date of birth and the current date. The result is broken down into years, months, and days. The calculation accurately handles leap years and the varying number of days in each month to give you a precise result.

What is Chronological Age?

Chronological age is the amount of time that has passed from your birth to a given date. It's the most common way of expressing age and is used for everything from legal matters to tracking developmental milestones. This tool calculates your precise chronological age.
