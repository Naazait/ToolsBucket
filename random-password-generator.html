Tips for a Strong Password

  • Length is Key: Aim for at least 16 characters. Every additional character makes a password exponentially harder to crack.
  • Mix It Up: Use a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid the Obvious: Never use personal information like your name, birthday, or common words like "password" or "123456".
  • Be Unique: Use a different, strong password for every important account.
