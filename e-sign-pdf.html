About Our Free eSign PDF Tool

What Does This Tool Do?

Our eSign PDF tool is a secure, browser-based application that allows you to add a legally binding electronic signature to any PDF document. In a world that is increasingly digital, the need to sign contracts, agreements, and forms remotely has become essential. This tool eliminates the cumbersome process of printing, signing by hand, and scanning. Instead, you can create a digital version of your signature in one of three ways: by drawing it with your mouse or finger, typing it out and choosing a handwritten font, or uploading a high-quality image of your signature. Once created, you can place this signature anywhere on your PDF document, resize it, and download the final, signed file instantly. The entire process is designed to be fast, intuitive, and, most importantly, completely private, as your files are never uploaded to a server.

How to Sign Your PDF Online

  1. Upload Your PDF: Begin by dragging your PDF file into the upload box, or simply click the box to select the file from your computer or mobile device.
  2. Create Your Signature: Once the document is displayed, click the "Add Signature" button. A pop-up will appear where you can choose to draw your signature, type it, or upload an image.
  3. Place and Adjust Signature: After creating your signature, it will appear on your document. Click and drag the signature to move it to the correct location. You can also resize it by dragging its corners.
  4. Apply and Download: When you are satisfied with the placement, click the "Apply & Continue" button. You will then be prompted to download your newly signed, secure PDF.

Benefits of Using an Online Signature Tool

  • Speed and Efficiency: Sign and send documents in minutes from anywhere in the world, without needing a printer or scanner.
  • Legally Binding: Electronic signatures created with this tool are legally recognized in many jurisdictions, making them valid for contracts, invoices, and official forms.
  • Enhanced Security: Your documents are processed locally in your browser and are never uploaded, ensuring maximum confidentiality and privacy.
  • Cost-Effective and Eco-Friendly: Save money on paper, ink, and postage while contributing to a greener environment by reducing paper waste.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is it secure to sign my documents with this tool?

Yes, absolutely. This tool is built for privacy. All processing, including file uploading, signature creation, and document signing, happens directly in your web browser. Your documents are never uploaded to a server, ensuring they remain confidential.

Are electronic signatures legally binding?

Electronic signatures are legally recognized in many countries around the world, including the United States (ESIGN Act) and the European Union (eIDAS Regulation). Signatures created with this tool are a valid form of electronic signature. However, for highly sensitive documents, you should consult with a legal professional to ensure you meet all specific requirements.

Can I use this on my mobile phone?

Yes, our eSign tool is fully responsive and works on any device with a modern web browser, including mobile phones and tablets. You can easily draw your signature with your finger or a stylus.

What happens to my document after I sign it?

After you sign your document, you are provided with a download link for the newly signed PDF. Your original file and the signed version remain on your device and are never stored by us. Once you close the browser tab, all data is gone.
