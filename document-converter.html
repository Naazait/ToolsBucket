How to Use the PDF Converter

  1. Upload Your File: Drag and drop your file onto the upload area, or click to select a `.txt`, `.jpg`, or `.png` file from your device.
  2. Convert: Click the "Convert to PDF" button. The tool will process your file locally in your browser.
  3. Download: Once the conversion is complete, a "Download PDF" button will appear. Click it to save your new file.
