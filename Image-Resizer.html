How Our Image Resizer Works

  1. Select Your Image: Click the "Select Image" button to upload a photo from your device.
  2. Enter New Dimensions: Input your desired new width or height in pixels. The other dimension will update automatically to preserve the aspect ratio. You can click the lock icon to disable this feature.
  3. Choose Format & Quality: Select your preferred output format (PNG, JPG, or WEBP). For JPG/WEBP, you can adjust the quality to balance file size and visual clarity.
  4. Download: Click "Resize & Download" to save the newly sized image to your device instantly.
